My First Day at CSSI

My first day at CSSI was amazing, I ddi not expect it to go the way it did. I expected to come to CSSI and not fit in, but I noticed that the classroom was a judgment free zone and that I could be myself. I met some of the coolest people and I did not feel like I was behind during any of the lessons that were taught. Even though I needed help at times, I was also able to help others and look forward to attending the rest of the program.
